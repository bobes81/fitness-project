Welcome to Your Fitness Plan!

Here, I aim to help you achieve the best possible results based on your body mass index (BMI). Using the BMI calculator, we will select the most suitable workout plans for you. The plans are divided into five categories, each tailored to different BMI results:

  • Underweight: If your BMI indicates that you're underweight, we’ll focus on strength-building exercises to help you gain muscle mass.
  • Normal weight: For those in the healthy BMI range, we’ll focus on a balanced workout plan that includes a mix of strength training and cardio.
  • Overweight: If you fall into the overweight category, we’ll prioritize exercises that help burn fat, improve cardiovascular health, and promote lean muscle development.
  • Obese: For individuals in this category, the goal is to start with low-impact exercises and focus on gradual weight loss while improving overall health.
  • Muscle Building: For those aiming to build muscle mass, we’ll provide a more intense workout plan with heavy lifting and targeted exercises.

Each category will have exercises and plans designed specifically to meet your goals.
